About

Running a Marathon can seem like an impossible task and you might not know where to start, thats why we are here to help! We have put together a 16 week comprehensive training plan to get you to your 42.2km goal. During the 16 weeks you will build your stamina and distance at a pace that suits you, the plan will cover active and rest days with time and distance goals for each planned run. The plan is designed to be a 16 week minimum training guide, if you find any of the weeks particularly difficult you can always repeat that week until you are ready to move on to the next. We reaslise this is a huge commitment to make so we have provided week 1 of the training plan to give you an idea of whats in store if you choose to take the plunge.
